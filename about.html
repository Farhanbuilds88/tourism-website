About Gilgit Baltistan

Discover the culture, beauty, and adventure of northern Pakistan.

The Heaven of Pakistan

Gilgit Baltistan is one of the most beautiful regions of Pakistan, famous for mountains, lakes, rivers, and adventure tourism. It is home to some of the world’s highest peaks including K2.

Thousands of tourists visit every year to enjoy trekking, camping, cultural festivals, and natural beauty.
